## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
StopGuard is your digital witness during traffic stops. Record audio, get real-time transcription, and receive instant analysis of whether your rights are being violated — all based on your state's specific laws.

ONE-TAP RECORDING
Start recording with a single tap. StopGuard captures audio, logs your GPS location, and begins transcribing the interaction in real-time. Your recording is automatically backed up to the cloud when you create an account.

REAL-TIME RIGHTS ANALYSIS
As the conversation unfolds, StopGuard's rights engine monitors for 8 categories of potential violations:
• Officer fails to state reason for the stop
• Search without consent or probable cause (4th Amendment)
• Officer interfering with your right to record
• Officer refusing to provide name or badge number
• Questioning after you invoke your right to silence
• Detention exceeding reasonable time without justification
• Custodial interrogation without Miranda warning
• Unclear detention status — prompts you to ask if you're free to go

ALL 50 STATES + FEDERAL LAW
StopGuard includes a comprehensive database of recording consent laws (one-party vs. all-party), stop-and-identify statutes, officer identification requirements, and state-specific rights for all 50 states and DC. Switch states anytime — the rights engine adapts instantly.

22 LANGUAGES SUPPORTED
Live transcription works in English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, Russian, Arabic, Portuguese, Hindi, Filipino, Italian, Polish, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Farsi, and more.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
• Local-first storage — recordings stay on your device by default
• Optional cloud backup to your own account
• No third-party servers, no analytics, no tracking, no ads
• End-to-end encryption (TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest)
• Export all your data anytime as a JSON file
• Delete individual incidents or all data with one tap
• GDPR and CCPA compliant

POST-STOP CHECKLIST
After a traffic stop, StopGuard guides you through a 9-item checklist including how to file a complaint, preserve evidence, and document what happened.

KNOW YOUR RIGHTS TAB
Browse federal constitutional rights (4th, 5th, and 1st Amendment protections) and state-specific laws in an easy-to-read card format. Always available, even without recording.

ACCOUNT SYSTEM
Create a free account to enable cloud backup, access your incidents from any device, and export your data. Account deletion is always available — no lock-in.

PRIVACY FIRST
StopGuard does not collect biometrics, advertising IDs, contact lists, or analytics data. We don't share data with third parties. The only data we have is what you explicitly create — your recordings, transcripts, and account email.

IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTICE
StopGuard is a tool for recording and information. It is not a substitute for legal advice from a qualified attorney. Recording laws vary by state — the app displays your state's consent law, but you are responsible for knowing and complying with local recording laws. StopGuard does not provide legal representation or advice.
